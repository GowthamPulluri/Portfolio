Microstrip Patch Antenna with DGS

Designed a Microstrip Patch Antenna with Defected Ground Structure (DGS) on a Rogers 5880 substrate, incorporating an inverted U-slot on the patch. The antenna, measuring 54mm x 66mm, is optimized for enhanced performance, improving bandwidth and impedance matching.This design offers a compact and efficient solution for modern wireless communication applications.

Microstrip Patch Antenna Using SIW

Designed a Substrate Integrated Waveguide (SIW) microstrip patch antenna with a central slot on a 31 mm x 24 mm patch, utilizing Rogers 5880 substrate. The SIW structure was employed to enhance the antenna's performance by reducing radiation loss and improving power handling capability. The central slot on the patch contributed to enhanced bandwidth and resonance.
